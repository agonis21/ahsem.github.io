Ahsem is a Bengali-American artist based in New York City.

"What is the highest form of art?"

Intrigued by this simple question, Ahsem has delved into the realms of:

  • Writing
  • Visual Arts
  • Music Production
  • Video Editing
  • Programming
  • ...and many more!

As Ahsem continues to create more works in multiple mediums, the search for the highest form of art continues.
